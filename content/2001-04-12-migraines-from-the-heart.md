Have severe migraines? A hole in your heart could be the cause. British <A HREF="http://www.newscientist.com/dailynews/news.jsp?id=ns9999621">researchers</A> have found a link between chronic severe migraines and small openings in the upper chambers of the heart. These openings allow blood to bypass the lungs, which may cause a migraine.
